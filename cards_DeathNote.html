StoryLine:

Death Note is a psychological thriller anime and manga series created by Tsugumi Ohba and Takeshi Obata. The story follows Light Yagami, a brilliant but disillusioned high school student who discovers a mysterious notebook called the "Death Note." This notebook allows its user to kill anyone simply by writing their name while picturing their face. Light, adopting the alias "Kira," begins using the Death Note to eliminate criminals, aiming to create a utopian world where he rules as a god. However, his actions attract the attention of L, a genius detective determined to stop him. What follows is a gripping battle of wits, as Light and L engage in a tense game of cat and mouse, testing their intellect and morality. The series explores themes of justice, power, and the consequences of playing god.
