StoryLine:

The Disastrous Life of Saiki K. follows the life of Kusuo Saiki, a high school student born with extraordinary psychic abilities. From telekinesis and telepathy to time travel, his powers make him nearly invincible. However, instead of enjoying them, Saiki finds his abilities to be a constant source of annoyance. All he wants is to live a quiet, ordinary life, but his eccentric classmates and bizarre situations keep dragging him into chaos. Whether it's dealing with the overly enthusiastic Nendou, the airheaded Kaidou, or the self-obsessed Teruhashi, Saiki's attempts to stay unnoticed are hilariously thwarted. His deadpan reactions to absurd scenarios create a comedic masterpiece as he navigates daily life.
